Model Methodology & Citations
Partisan Lean (weight: 0.83)
2020 presidential vote share by district, sourced from Daily Kos Elections. PlanScore Unified District Model (2021) validates this as the dominant predictor across all 50 states (β = 0.83, 95% CI: 0.66–0.98). Rogers (2023) shows correlation between presidential vote and state house vote rose to 0.77+ in 2020–2022 cycle.

Incumbency (weight: 0.05)
Coded −1 (R incumbent), 0 (open seat), +1 (D incumbent). PlanScore β = 0.05 (95% CI: 0.02–0.07). Uppal (2010, Economics & Politics) found RDD-based incumbency effects in state legislatures are smaller than traditional estimates suggest. 96% of ND incumbents reelected in 2022 but this largely reflects partisan sorting, not personal advantage (Rogers 2023).
Contested Primary Effect (Hogan 2003)
Counter to conventional wisdom, Hogan (2003, American Politics Research) found contested primaries help state legislative candidates in general elections due to name recognition effects in low-information environments. This is opposite to the Fouirnaies & Hall (2020, Journal of Politics) finding for U.S. House/Senate, which showed ~6pt vote share decrease from divisive primaries.

Rare Events & Separation
With ~11.7% Democratic wins in ND House races, standard MLE produces biased estimates. Model applies Firth's penalized likelihood (Firth 1993, Biometrika; Heinze & Schemper 2002) and King-Zeng rare events correction (2001, Political Analysis). Probabilities are pre-primary baselines; update after June 9, 2026 primary.

"The Invite" (Day 2) → "The Scoreboard" (Day 4, live total inserted) → "The Clock" (Day 5, final total + 11 AM hard deadline). Each email serves a distinct psychological function: awareness, competition, urgency.

Day-by-Day Sequence
Day 1 — Sun Mar 1 · Warm Opener
Paul → personal email to ND credit union CEOs at 9:00 AM (high-trust, peer-to-peer). Jaymason → mirrors to SD CEOs same window. Sweepstakes opens at midnight. Responsible: John (Paul) · Kenley (Jaymason).

Day 3 — Tue Mar 3 · Memo + Trustee Solicitation
Kenley drafts internal memo: SD total raised, ND total raised, combined goal, "one week per year" framing — short and punchy. Paul + Jaymason send direct personal solicitation to CEOs (separate from memo). Responsible: Kenley (memo) · John (Paul) · Kenley (Jaymason).
🔥 Day 4 — Wed Mar 4 · Big Push Day 1
CC Email #2 "The Scoreboard" — Subject: "ND + SD update: here's where we stand." Show live combined total vs $10K goal (get figure from John or Kenley before sending). Competition framing, daily drawings reminder, single CTA. ND + SD Committee second wave to all staff. ⚠ Insert live dollar total before sending. Responsible: Lindsey (CC) · John (ND) · Kenley (SD).
🚨 Day 5 — Thu Mar 5 · Closes 11 AM EST
John sends short urgent memo nudge before CC blast. CC Email #3 "The Clock" — Subject: "ND + SD: closes at 11 AM today — last chance." Three paragraphs, one bold CTA, final dollar total inserted. Send before 9 AM. Paul + Jaymason final direct CEO push — be direct, last outreach. ⚠ Insert final dollar total before sending. Responsible: Lindsey (CC) · John · Kenley.
Day 6 — Fri Mar 6 · Thank You
George sends high-authority thank you to all donors. Acknowledges ND + SD participation, celebrates combined result vs $10K goal, ties to national $315K effort. Annie coordinates the send.
